Monarch Metal Custom Z Clip Configurator

How to use the configurator:
  • Fill up the required fields (Z Clip Option, Quantity, Length, Spacing)
  • Click "CALCULATE"
  • The answers will show up on a separate page, and you will be able to download the results in PDF
  • To reset the page, utilize the "Clear All Fields" button
  • Click the Monarch Metal logo on the results page to be redirected to the home page
  • For details like cut/punch charges, total inches, and setup fee, check the console (⋮ → More tools → Developer tools → Console)
  • For optimized full lengths, click the "Optimize Length" button to consider the saw blade thickness.
  • Red: Optimization is on
  • Black: Optimization is off

Please note that all measurements are in inches
